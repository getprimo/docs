title: "Track SaaS expenses"
description: "Monitor your SaaS spending with contract values, license costs, and unused license tracking to optimize your software budget."
---

Primo helps you track SaaS expenses across your organization. By configuring contract and license information for each application, you can monitor total spend, identify unused licenses, and optimize costs from the dashboard.

## Dashboard overview

The **Expense Management** card on the Primo dashboard shows a summary of your SaaS spending:

- **Total actual cost** — The sum of annual contract values across all your SaaS applications
- **Total unused cost** — The monetary value of licenses that are paid for but not assigned to any user
- **Total unused slots** — The number of license seats available but not in use

This gives you instant visibility into where your SaaS budget is going and where you can save money by reclaiming unused licenses.

## Configure contract information

To track expenses for a SaaS application, you need to configure its contract details:

1. In the Primo console, go to **Apps**.
2. Select the application you want to configure.
3. Open the **Settings** tab.
4. Under **Contract & Licenses**, configure the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Pricing mode** | Choose between *Per licence* (cost per user) or *Flat rate* (fixed annual cost) |
| **Currency** | The currency for this contract (USD, EUR, GBP) |
| **Billing frequency** | How often you are billed (monthly, quarterly, annually) |
| **Annual contract value** | The total yearly cost (for flat rate pricing) |
| **Start date** | When the contract began |
| **Renewal date** | When the contract renews |

<Note>
For applications with per-license pricing, Primo automatically calculates the annual contract value based on your license count and cost per license.
</Note>

## Track license usage

Once contract information is configured, Primo tracks license usage by comparing:

- **Billed licenses** — The number of licenses you're paying for
- **Active identities** — The number of users with accounts in the application

The difference between these numbers represents your **unused licenses** — seats you're paying for but not using.

## Optimize your spending

Use the expense tracking data to:

- **Identify unused licenses** — Reclaim or cancel licenses that aren't being used
- **Forecast renewal costs** — Plan your budget with visibility into upcoming renewals
- **Negotiate contracts** — Use actual usage data when renegotiating with vendors
- **Right-size subscriptions** — Adjust your license count based on actual needs
