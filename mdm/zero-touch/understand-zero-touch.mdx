# Why Zero-Touch Deployment Matters

ZTD is becoming increasingly important in today's fast-paced, remote-work environments. It offers several key advantages:

## 1. Enhanced Security

With ZTD, Mobile Device Management (MDM) software is deeply integrated into the device from the start. This means:

- The MDM cannot be removed, ensuring consistent security policies.
- Devices remain enrolled in the MDM even if wiped, maintaining control over company assets.

## 2. Improved Employee Experience

ZTD significantly enhances the onboarding process for new employees:

- Devices are pre-configured with necessary apps and settings before they reach the user.
- Employees can start working immediately, without waiting for IT setup.
- Consistent experience across all devices in the organization.

## 3. Increased Efficiency

ZTD offers substantial benefits for IT departments and the organization as a whole:

- Reduces the time and resources needed for device setup and configuration.
- Enables rapid deployment of devices, even in remote locations.
- Minimizes human error in the setup process.
