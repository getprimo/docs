This article explains when Zero-Touch Deployment (ZTD) is available for devices ordered through Primo, and how to configure Apple Business Manager (ABM) or Microsoft Entra so that devices are automatically enrolled on delivery.

## Understand why ZTD depends on local keyboards

Zero-Touch Deployment relies on supplier accreditations that are region-dependent:

- **Mac devices** require enrollment through **Apple Business Manager (ABM)**
- **Windows devices** require enrollment through **Microsoft Autopilot**

Only suppliers accredited in a given region can provide these services, and they are authorized to deliver devices **exclusively with the local keyboard configuration of that region**.

When a non-local keyboard is selected, the order is routed to a different supplier that is not accredited for ZTD — making Zero-Touch Deployment unavailable.

## Check ZTD availability by delivery region and keyboard

| Delivery region | Local keyboard | ZTD available | Non-local keyboard | ZTD available |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| EU | AZERTY in France; local QWERTY in EU | Yes | QWERTY US / UK / International | No |
| UK | QWERTY UK | Yes | AZERTY / QWERTY US / QWERTZ | No |
| US | QWERTY US | Yes | AZERTY / QWERTY UK / QWERTZ | No |

## Configure Apple Business Manager for ZTD

To automatically enroll Apple devices ordered through Primo in Zero-Touch, authorize the reseller to register devices in your ABM.

1. In Primo, go to [**Settings → Orders**](https://app.getprimo.com/settings/).
2. Under **Zero-Touch Deployment**, click **Apple**.
3. Choose the country(ies) for which you want to enable Zero-Touch procurement, and copy the Reseller ID(s).
4. Go to [Apple Business Manager](https://business.apple.com/), click your account name at the bottom left, and select **Preferences**.
5. Select **Organisation Information**. Copy your **Organisation ID** and paste it into the cockpit.
6. Under **Device Management Services**, select **Management Assignment**. Next to **Customer Numbers**, click **Edit**.
7. Add the Reseller ID(s).
8. In Primo, activate the toggle to finalize.

## Configure Microsoft Entra for ZTD

### Step 1: Authorize the reseller to register future devices

1. In Primo, go to [**Settings → Orders**](https://app.getprimo.com/settings/).
2. Under **Zero-Touch Procurement**, click **Windows**.
3. Select the country or countries where you want to enable Autopilot procurement, then click **Authorize our reseller**.
4. Follow the displayed instructions.
5. A confirmation screen will indicate that the partner relationship has been successfully created.

### Step 2: Enter Microsoft Entra information

1. Log in to the [Azure portal](https://portal.azure.com/) and search for **Microsoft Entra ID**.
2. Copy your **Tenant ID** and **Primary Domain** and paste them into the cockpit.
3. Activate the configuration toggle to finalize the integration.

## Select keyboard layout when ordering

When ordering a device through Primo, keyboard selection determines ZTD eligibility.

### Local keyboard (recommended)

Automatically applies the standard keyboard for the delivery country. Ensures the order is handled by a ZTD-authorized supplier.

Examples:
- France → AZERTY
- Belgium → QWERTY BE
- Germany → QWERTZ DE
- United Kingdom → QWERTY UK
- United States → QWERTY US

### Specific keyboard layout

Available options: QWERTY US, QWERTY UK, QWERTY International, QWERTZ, Spanish QWERTY, AZERTY.

<Warning>
Selecting a keyboard layout that is **not local to the delivery country disables Zero Touch Deployment**.
</Warning>

## Common ordering scenarios

- **Delivery to the United States**
  - Local keyboard → ZTD available
  - QWERTY International or AZERTY → ZTD not available

- **Delivery to France (EU)**
  - Local keyboard (AZERTY) → ZTD available
  - QWERTY US or QWERTY International → ZTD not available

- **Delivery to France with a requirement for QWERTY US keyboards**
  - ZTD is not available through Primo's standard ordering flow

## Frequently asked questions

### Why is ZTD unavailable with QWERTY International in France?

ZTD depends on supplier accreditations tied to specific regions. In France, only suppliers providing AZERTY keyboards are accredited for Apple ABM or Microsoft Autopilot. Selecting a non-local keyboard routes the order to a different supplier that is not authorized for ZTD in that region.

### Can I order devices in France with QWERTY US keyboards?

Yes, two options are available:

1. **Place a standard order without ZTD** — select QWERTY US. Devices will be delivered ready to use, without Zero-Touch Deployment.
2. **Submit a special request (longer lead time)** — add a comment to your order requesting **"QWERTY US + ABM"**. Primo will check availability with suppliers. Expected lead time: **3 to 4 weeks**.

### Does selecting "AZERTY" always enable ZTD?

No. If you manually select AZERTY for a delivery **outside France**, ZTD will not be available. The order will not be handled by a France-based, ZTD-authorized supplier. To ensure ZTD eligibility, always select **Local keyboard** rather than a specific layout.
