# Introduction

This article explains why **Zero Touch Deployment (ZTD)** is only available when ordering devices with the **local keyboard of the delivery country** on Primo. It also outlines how keyboard selection impacts ZTD eligibility and what options are available if you require a non-local keyboard layout.

# Understand why Zero Touch Deployment depends on local keyboards

Zero Touch Deployment relies on supplier-specific accreditations that are **region-dependent**:

- **Mac devices** require enrollment through **Apple Business Manager (ABM)**
- **Windows devices** require enrollment through **Microsoft Autopilot**

Only suppliers accredited in a given region can provide these services. These suppliers are authorized to deliver devices **exclusively with the local keyboard configuration of that region**.

When a non-local keyboard is selected, the order is routed to a different supplier that is **not accredited for ZTD**, which makes Zero Touch Deployment unavailable.

Learn more: [Device Orders with Zero-Touch](/mdm/zero-touch/device-orders-zero-touch)

# Check ZTD availability by delivery region and keyboard type

| Delivery region | Local keyboard | ZTD available | Non-local keyboard | ZTD available |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| EU | AZERTY in France; Local QWERTY in EU | Yes | QWERTY US / UK / International | No |
| UK | QWERTY UK | Yes | AZERTY / QWERTY US / QWERTZ | No |
| US | QWERTY US | Yes | AZERTY / QWERTY UK / QWERTZ | No |

# Configure keyboard selection in Primo

When ordering a device through Primo (during onboarding or from the catalog), follow the steps below.

## Select the delivery region

Choose the region where the device will be delivered: **EU** / **UK** / **US**

## Choose the keyboard layout

You can select one of the following options.

## Select a local keyboard (recommended for ZTD)

This option automatically applies the standard keyboard for the delivery country.

Examples:

- France → AZERTY
- Belgium → QWERTY BE
- Germany → QWERTY DE
- United Kingdom → QWERTY UK
- United States → QWERTY US

Selecting a local keyboard ensures the order is handled by a ZTD-authorized supplier.

## Select a specific keyboard layout

Available options include:

- QWERTY US
- QWERTY UK
- QWERTY International
- QWERTZ
- Spanish QWERTY
- AZERTY

<Warning>
Selecting a keyboard layout that is **not local to the delivery country disables Zero Touch Deployment**.
</Warning>

# Review common ordering scenarios

- **Delivery to the United States**
  - Local keyboard → ZTD available
  - QWERTY International or AZERTY → ZTD not available

- **Delivery to France (EU)**
  - Local keyboard (AZERTY) → ZTD available
  - QWERTY US or QWERTY International → ZTD not available

- **Delivery to France with a requirement for QWERTY US keyboards**
  - ZTD is not available through Primo's standard ordering flow

# Resolve frequent questions

## Understand why ZTD is unavailable with QWERTY International in France

ZTD depends on supplier accreditations tied to specific regions.

In France, only suppliers providing **AZERTY keyboards** are accredited for Apple ABM or Microsoft Autopilot.

Selecting a non-local keyboard routes the order to a different supplier that is not authorized for ZTD in that region.

## Order devices in France with QWERTY US keyboards

You have two options:

1. **Place a standard order without ZTD**
   - Select QWERTY US. Devices will be delivered ready to use, without Zero Touch Deployment.

2. **Submit a special request (longer lead time)**
   - Add a comment to your order requesting **"QWERTY US + ABM"**.
   - Primo will check availability with suppliers.
   - Expected lead time: **3 to 4 weeks**.

## Confirm whether selecting "AZERTY" always enables ZTD

No.

If you manually select **AZERTY** for a delivery **outside France**, ZTD will not be available. The order will not be handled by a France-based, ZTD-authorized supplier.

To ensure ZTD eligibility, always select **Local keyboard** instead of choosing a specific layout.
