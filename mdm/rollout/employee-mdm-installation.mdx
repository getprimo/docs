This article will guide you step by step through installing Primo on your device. This installation is crucial for your company's fleet management.

**Some context before we begin:**

- **Primo** is the tool your company uses to track, manage, and secure its devices.
- **FleetDM** is the Mobile Device Management (MDM) solution deployed by Primo.

The procedure is simple and quick: it takes **less than 2 minutes**.

<Warning>
  **Important**: You must have **administrator rights** on the device to complete the installation. If you do not have them, contact your administrator before proceeding.
</Warning>

## Step 1: Access the Primo enrollment space

You can access the Primo enrollment space in two different ways.

### Option 1: Access the enrollment from the invitation email

If you received an enrollment email titled **"Invite to join \<Company Name\> on Primo"**:

1. Open the enrollment email.
2. Click **Enroll my computer on Primo**.
3. You are redirected to the Primo enrollment web page.

### Option 2: Access the enrollment from a direct link

If your administrator shared an enrollment link with you: open the link and you are directly redirected to the Primo enrollment web page.

## Step 2: Authenticate

Once on the Primo enrollment page:

1. Enter your professional email address (or your personal email address if you are new to the company).
2. Enter the **6-digit verification code** sent to your email address.

## Step 3: Start the installation

After logging in, Primo will prompt you to install the MDM on your device.

1. Choose one of the following options:
   - If a device is already assigned to you, select it and click **Enroll**.
   - If no device is assigned to you, click **Deploy MDM on another device**.
2. Select your device OS (Windows, MacOS, Linux, iOS, Android) and download installer.
3. Follow the installation instructions corresponding to your operating system.

### Installation on macOS

1. Install the agent to your device
   1. Locate the file **primo-enrollment.mobileconfig** downloaded to your device
   2. Double-click the file. A system message confirms that the profile has been downloaded and must now be installed.
1. Install the profile to your device
   1. Open **System Settings** and go to **General \> Device Management** (You can also use the search field in System Settings and search for Device Management.)
   2. Select the profile in the list and double-click on "**\<Your Company\> Enrollment**"
   3. Click **Enroll** at the bottom left of the window.
   4. Enter your **administrator password** to confirm the installation.

<details>
  <summary>Click here in case of error during the agent installation</summary>

  If you see an error that primo-enrollment.pkg is blocked:

  You can unblock it in Settings > Privacy & Security > Security
</details>

The enrollment is complete. You will receive a confirmation email.

### Installation on Windows

1. Double-click on the "**primo-enrollment.msi**" file that has been downloaded
2. A Windows security window opens, continue by clicking on **"Yes"**
3. The enrollment is complete. You will receive a confirmation email.

### Installation on iOS

1. Go to **Settings** \> **General** \> **Device Management**
2. Select and **install the profile** MDM Primo
3. **Confirm the installation** by entering your unlock code if prompted

### Installation on Android

1. Follow the Android enrollment steps
2. When prompted for login credentials, enter your Primo login information
