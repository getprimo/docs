Installing the MDM is a crucial step in fleet management. This procedure is simple and quick: it takes only a few minutes and is mandatory for all users.

## Deploy the MDM to your employees

To deploy the MDM on your employees' devices, you can send MDM deployment invitations (called "enrollment") from your Primo cockpit.

- **Send invitation to 1 employee:** click on the employee's profile then click on "Invite to MDM"
- **Send invitations to a pool of employees:** select the desired employees then click on "Invite to MDM" then "Invite X selected employees".
- **Send invitations to all employees:** click on "Invite to MDM" then "Invite matching employees".

## Installation Information

- **Receiving the invitation email from Primo:** once the enrollment is initiated from the Primo cockpit, your employees receive an email from Primo (support@getprimo.com): "Secure your equipment with Primo".
- The invitation email contains a **personal link** that allows each employee to install the agent on their computer. We have also integrated an installation guide detailing step by step the MDM installation process.

<Note>
The information contained in the invitation email is valid for 7 days. After this period, you will need to send a new enrollment invitation.
</Note>
