Installing the MDM is a crucial step in fleet management. This procedure is simple and quick: it takes only a few minutes and is mandatory for all users.

## Enrollment options

Primo supports three ways to enroll a device into MDM:

### Option 1 — Email invitation

Send a personalized enrollment email directly from the Primo cockpit. Each employee receives a unique link to install the MDM agent on their device.

**Send an invitation:**
- **To 1 employee:** open the employee's profile, then click **Invite to MDM**.
- **To a selection of employees:** select the desired employees, click **Invite to MDM**, then **Invite X selected employees**.
- **To all employees:** click **Invite to MDM**, then **Invite matching employees**.

Once initiated, employees receive an email from Primo (support@getprimo.com) with the subject "Secure your equipment with Primo". The email includes:
- A **personal enrollment link** unique to that employee.
- A step-by-step installation guide.

<Note>
The enrollment link is valid for **7 days**. After this period, you will need to send a new invitation.
</Note>

### Option 2 — Enrollment URL

Each employee has a dedicated enrollment page accessible via a personal URL. You can share this URL directly (e.g. via Slack or your intranet) as an alternative to email.

The URL can be found in the employee's profile in the Primo cockpit.

### Option 3 — Downloadable packages

For managed deployments or environments where email is not suitable, Primo provides downloadable installer packages (macOS `.pkg`, Windows `.exe`) that can be distributed via your existing software deployment tools (e.g. Jamf, SCCM, or manual installation).

Packages can be downloaded from the enrollment section of the Primo cockpit and deployed silently without requiring employee interaction.
