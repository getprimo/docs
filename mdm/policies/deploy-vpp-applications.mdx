---
title: "Set up VPP (Apple Volume Purchase Program)"
description: "Connect your Apple Business Manager Volume Purchase Program (VPP) token to enable App Store app deployment on macOS, iOS, and iPadOS devices."
---

**VPP (Volume Purchase Program)** is part of Apple Business Manager (ABM). It allows organizations to purchase App Store app licenses in bulk and deploy them centrally to macOS, iOS, and iPadOS devices — without requiring individual Apple IDs from employees.

Once VPP is connected, you can deploy App Store apps directly from **MDM > Software**. See:
- [Deploy apps (macOS, Windows, Linux)](/mdm/policies/manage-apps-laptop) — for macOS App Store apps
- [Deploy apps (iOS, iPadOS & Android)](/mdm/policies/manage-apps-smartphone) — for iOS/iPadOS App Store apps

---

## Prerequisites

- An **Apple Business Manager (ABM)** account
- Zero-Touch Deployment activated — connect your ABM under [MDM Settings](https://app.getprimo.com/settings/?tab=mdm)

---

## Connect your VPP token

### Step 1: Generate a VPP token in ABM

1. In ABM, go to the **Sites** section.
2. Create a new site if you don't have one yet.
3. Generate a **VPP token** from that site and download it.

### Step 2: Import the token

1. In Primo, go to **MDM > Software > Add app > App Store (VPP)**.
2. Select the **"No Team"** scope.
3. Upload the VPP token file.

Once imported, your purchased App Store apps will be available to deploy from **MDM > Software**.

---

## Add licenses in ABM

Before deploying an app, make sure you have enough licenses assigned:

1. In ABM, go to **Apps and Books**.
2. Search for the app and purchase the required number of licenses.
3. Assign the licenses to the site linked to your VPP token.

Licenses can be reassigned at any time when a device is unenrolled.

---

## Migration from another MDM

If you are switching from another MDM that already uses VPP:

1. Add your apps first (following the steps in the deployment guides above).
2. Ask your previous MDM provider to **remove its VPP link**.
3. This may cause VPP-managed apps to be **temporarily removed** from devices.
4. Primo will automatically redeploy all configured apps once the new VPP link is active.

<Warning>
Never share the same VPP token between two MDMs simultaneously. Doing so will cause conflicts and may result in apps being removed from devices.
</Warning>
