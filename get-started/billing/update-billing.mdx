---

This article explains how administrators can access and update billing information for their Primo account.

<Note>
Only users with **administrator permissions** can view and manage billing information in Primo. If you do not see the Billing section, verify that your user account has administrator rights.
</Note>

To update your billing information in Primo:

## 1. Access the Billing section

1. Open the **Primo cockpit**.
2. Go to **Settings > Billing**.

All available billing profiles linked to your organization are displayed.

## 2. Select a billing profile

1. Review the list of billing profiles.
2. Select the billing profile you want to update.

You are redirected to the **Hyperline billing portal** associated with that profile.

## 3. Update billing details

1. In the Hyperline billing portal, click **Edit details**.
2. Update your payment or billing information as needed.
3. Save your changes to apply them.

<Tip>
About billing email address: you can configure **multiple billing email addresses** for a single billing profile.
</Tip>
