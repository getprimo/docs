This article explains where to find your Primo invoices and what information they contain. It helps you access your billing history and understand the details included in each invoice.

# Understand invoice content

Primo invoices are official documents summarizing your charges. Depending on the billing type, invoices may cover subscription fees or hardware orders.

## Subscription invoices

Subscription invoices include:

- The billing period
- Billed products and quantities
- The total amount, with a breakdown of HT / VAT / TTC
- Primo (Clutch) legal information
- Invoice due date

## Device and hardware invoices

Invoices related to device orders include:

- Hardware reference
- Ordered quantity
- The total amount, with a breakdown of HT / VAT / TTC
- Primo (Clutch) legal information
- Invoice due date

# Access your invoices

## Access invoices from the Primo interface

<Note>
Only users with administrator permissions can access billing information from the Primo cockpit.
</Note>

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**.
2. Review the available billing profiles, including:
   - The profile used for subscription payments
   - The profiles used for device orders and shipments
3. Select a billing profile to be redirected to the Hyperline billing portal.

From the Hyperline portal, you can access all invoices linked to the selected billing profile and check their status:

- Paid
- Payment in progress
- Late

## Receive invoices by email

Invoices are also sent by email to the billing email address associated with the account.

Multiple billing email addresses can be configured, allowing several recipients to receive invoices.
