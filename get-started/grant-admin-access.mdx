title: "Grant administrator access to Primo"
description: "To grant administrator rights in Primo, promote an existing collaborator via the Employees tab or import a new collaborator using CSV, ensuring they are marked with a star icon in the Employees list to identify their admin status."
---

This article explains how to grant administrator rights to your Primo cockpit. This allows them to view, configure, and manage your company's Primo workspace.

# Promote an existing collaborator to admin

1. Go to the **Employees** tab in the Primo cockpit.
2. Search for and select the relevant collaborator.
3. Click **Actions** in the top right corner.
4. Select **Promote to admin**.

The collaborator now has admin rights in Primo.

# Add a collaborator who is not yet in the cockpit

If the collaborator is not listed under Employees:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Employee Sync**.
2. Click **Import from CSV**.
3. Select **Or click here to copy paste table data**.
4. Paste the following into the table:

```
First name,Last name,Email
Clémentine,Cruz,support@getprimo.com
```

*(Adapt the details as needed)*

5. Complete the import. The collaborator will now appear under **Employees**.
6. Then follow "Promote an existing collaborator to admin" to promote them to admin.

# Identify admins in the cockpit

In the Employees list, collaborators with administrator access are marked with a star icon ⭐.

<Note>
**Access levels**

At the moment, different administrator levels are not supported. This is on our product roadmap.
</Note>
