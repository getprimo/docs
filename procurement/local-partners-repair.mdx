## What is Reevive?

Reevive is a cutting-edge platform designed to empower individuals and businesses to repair and maintain their electronic devices. By providing easy-to-follow guides, tools, and resources, Reevive aims to extend the lifespan of electronics and reduce e-waste.

## The Benefits of Using Reevive

- **Cost-effective:** Repairing devices is often much cheaper than buying new ones.
- **Environmentally friendly:** By extending the life of electronics, we reduce e-waste and conserve resources.
- **Skill development:** Users learn valuable technical skills through the repair process.
- **Customization:** Repairing allows for personalization and upgrades to devices.

## How Reevive Works With Primo

Reevive offers a straightforward process to get your devices repaired:

1. **Access device modal:** Navigate to your device in Primo and select it.
2. **Request repair:** Click on the "repair" option within the device modal.
3. **Complete the form:** Fill out the repair request form with details about the issue you're experiencing.
4. **Review quote:** Reevive will analyze your request and send you a repair quote.
5. **Decision:** You can choose to accept or decline the quote with no obligation.
