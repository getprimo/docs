## Where does Primo currently ship?

Primo is currently able to ship hardware devices in the following regions:

- **Europe**
- **United Kingdom**
- **United States**

These are the countries where our hardware ordering and delivery services are fully live today.

## Expanding to more countries

Our goal is to continuously expand Primo's shipping coverage and add more countries over time.

We are actively working on opening new regions, and we already have logistics and distribution partners in several additional countries. This means we may be able to support new markets in the short term.

You can find the current list of partner-supported countries here: [link to partner supported countries spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1LMK2YXAKmmQaW4KWVwNHI3fAwVXXvwN7joDyzXbq07I/edit?gid=1135924200#gid=1135924200)

## Requesting support for a new country

If the country you need is not yet available, customers and partners can request coverage by contacting us at:

[support@getprimo.com](mailto:support@getprimo.com)

Please include the country (and any relevant context about expected demand), and our team will do its best to prioritize the request and assess how quickly we can enable shipping there.
