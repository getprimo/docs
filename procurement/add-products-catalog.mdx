At Primo, we strive to save you valuable time when purchasing IT equipment and accessories. While we continuously update our catalog, you may occasionally find that certain items are not yet listed.

## Regional Catalogs

Primo maintains separate catalogs for different geographical regions:

- Europe (EU)
- United Kingdom (UK)
- United States (US)

## How to Add a Missing Item

If you can't find what you're looking for, follow these simple steps:

1. Access your Primo catalog
2. Click the "Add" button in the top right corner
3. Fill in the product details, including:
   - Complete product name and model number
   - Manufacturer information
   - Technical specifications
   - **Your geographical region** (Europe, UK, or US)

Our team will review your submission and work to add the requested item to the appropriate regional catalog as quickly as possible.
