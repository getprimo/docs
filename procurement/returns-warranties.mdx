At Primo, we aim to ensure a seamless experience for managing returns and warranty claims. Below are the details on how to handle returns and repairs for products purchased through us.

## 1. Return Conditions

- **Return Window**: Customers have **14 days** from the delivery date to request a return.
- **Product Condition**:
  - **Unopened Products**: Returns are accepted under standard conditions.
  - **Opened Products**: Returns are only accepted in specific cases, such as when the wrong product was delivered.
- **How to Start a Return**: Log in to your **Primo platform** to initiate the return process.

## 2. Damaged Products

If the product is damaged, please upload **clear photos of the damage** when opening a claim on the Primo platform.

## 3. Warranty Information

All products come with a **1-year manufacturer warranty**. You can contact the manufacturer directly for guidance on warranty claims.

### Manufacturer Contact Details:

Here is a list of key manufacturers and their contact information:

- **Apple**: Check your device coverage with [this link](https://checkcoverage.apple.com/).
- **Dell**: Support Services & Warranty with [this link](https://www.dell.com/support/contractservices/en-us).
- **HP**: Support Services & Warranty with [this link](https://support.hp.com/fr-fr/check-warranty).
- **iiyama**: Support Services & Warranty with [this link](https://iiyama.com/gl_en/support#product-support). They usually offer 3 years of warranty.

For other manufacturers, please contact Primo at [support@getprimo.com](mailto:support@getprimo.com).

For Apple repairs, Primo provides a list of authorized and non-authorized repair providers based on your request. Submit your repair request, and we will return to you with a quote.

## 4. International Customers

Returns and repairs are also available for international customers. Please note that conditions may vary by country, especially regarding **shipping costs**, which may increase for certain regions.

## 5. Need Assistance?

If you need help or have questions, please contact our support team at [support@getprimo.com](mailto:support@getprimo.com). This service is available to assist you in special cases.
